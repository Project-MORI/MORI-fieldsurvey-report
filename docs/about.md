# Site Policy/Disclaimer 

## Project Overview
This website is part of "Project for improving control of illegal deforestation through advanced SAR and AI technologies in the Brazilian Amazon", **Project MORI**, a JICA technical cooperation project implemented jointly with **IBAMA** and other partner institutions in Brazil.

**Project MORI**  
[https://github.com/Project-MORI](https://github.com/Project-MORI)

**ODA Project Site**
[https://www.jica.go.jp/oda/project/1900371/index.html](https://www.jica.go.jp/oda/project/1900371/index.html)

The project aims to strengthen environmental monitoring and enforcement capacity through the integration of satellite remote sensing using SAR, AI-based deforestation prediction, and field-survey data.

## Data Sources and Attribution

This MORI Field Survey Report uses multiple open and institutional datasets. All datasets are credited to their original providers. Users referencing this website or derived products should ensure proper citation of each dataset as required by the respective provider.

### **ALOS-2 (PALSAR-2) – JAXA**
- Provider: Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA)  
- Official site: https://www.eorc.jaxa.jp/ALOS-2/en/  
- Notes: Data used under JAXA’s permitted research frameworks.

### **JJ-FAST – JICA / JAXA**
- Providers: Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) & JAXA  
- Official site: https://www.eorc.jaxa.jp/jjfast/  
- Notes: Deforestation early-warning system jointly developed by JICA and JAXA.

### **DETER – INPE**
- Provider: Instituto Nacional de Pesquisas Espaciais (INPE)  
- Official site: https://terrabrasilis.dpi.inpe.br/  
- Notes: Near real-time deforestation detection; public open data. (CC BY-SA 4.0)

### **PRODES – INPE**
- Provider: INPE  
- Official site: https://terrabrasilis.dpi.inpe.br/  
- Notes: Annual official deforestation statistics for the Brazilian Amazon. (CC BY-SA 4.0)

### **NASA FIRMS – Fire Information for Resource Management System**
- Provider: NASA / NOAA  
- Official site: https://firms.modaps.eosdis.nasa.gov/  
- Notes: Active fire alerts from MODIS and VIIRS satellites.

### **Embargo Dataset – IBAMA**
- Provider: Instituto Brasileiro do Meio Ambiente e dos Recursos Naturais Renováveis (IBAMA)  
- Official site: https://dados.gov.br/dataset/areas-embargadas  
- Notes: Administrative embargo areas; publicly available.

### **Sentinel-2 (for NDVI) – ESA Copernicus Program**
- Provider: European Space Agency (ESA)  
- Copernicus Open Access Hub: https://dataspace.copernicus.eu/  
- Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel-2 data (ESA, 2015-2025)
- Notes: Data used under the Copernicus license.

### **Landsat (for NDVI) - USGS and NASA** 
- Provider: USGS (United States Geological Survey) and NASA
- USGS Landsat Missions: https://www.usgs.gov/landsat-missions
- Landsat data courtesy of the U.S. Geological Survey.
- Notes: Data used Landsat Open Data Policy

### **Planet NICFI Basemaps and daily imaginary – Planet Labs PBC**
- Provider: Planet Labs under the NICFI (Norway’s International Climate and Forest Initiative) program  
- Official site: https://www.planet.com/nicfi/  
- Notes: High-resolution monthly basemaps used for visual interpretation.
